Our Story

Food that feels close to home

We are inspired by the joy of a table full of flavour, where every plate feels generous, aromatic, and made with real care.

ZaikaX is built around one simple idea: good food should instantly pull people in. The aroma of spices, the richness of slow-cooked gravies, the comfort of warm breads, and the satisfaction of a complete meal all matter to us.

Our kitchen focuses on dishes people truly crave, from smoky tandoori bites and flavorful curries to biryanis that feel festive and filling. Every order is prepared to bring restaurant-style taste to everyday meals.

Whether someone is ordering for lunch, dinner, a family gathering, or a late-night craving, we want every ZaikaX meal to feel tasty, reliable, and memorable enough to order again.
